Flynnzo's

A pizza company by Daniel Flynn

Order Online Now!

Flynnzo's started with a dream. The dream was a vision of the perfect food; the single meal that could unite the world. The circle is a symbol of unity, an unbroken chain. The Ouroboros of mealtime. Here we make that dream a reality. Through a crispy crust portal we manifest the fundamental elements of zesty sauce, melted cheese, woodfire smoke, and tender herbs. Like an artifact from advanced civilization lost to time, pizza is both a time honored tradition and futuristic technology. We harness this power to make the world a better place, one savory triangle at a time. Earth isn't flat after all - it's pizza shaped.
